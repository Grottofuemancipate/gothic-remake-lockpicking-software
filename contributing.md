## ❓ What problem does this mod solve?

Gothic's lockpicking mechanic has always divided players. Some see it as immersive hardcore gameplay, others just want to open chests and get loot without wrestling with a spring lock.

**Two modes, freely switchable:**

✦ **Full Auto Mode** — Press the lockpick button, the chest opens instantly. Skip the entire lockpicking minigame.

✦ **Show Angle Mode** — Keep the lockpick interface, but display a marker on screen showing exactly how many degrees to turn the screwdriver.

The developer's friend — Alrik — is "lockpick-challenged". Every time he failed, he'd smash his mouse in frustration. To make the game experience smoother, this mod was made. Now he happily opens chests.
